# 7 Outscraper Alternatives for Google Maps Lead Generation (2026)

Outscraper does one thing really well: it scrapes Google Maps data on demand and lets you pay for exactly what you use. No monthly subscription, no quota resets, no wasted credits on a plan you only half-use.

The catch is the enrichment model. Base Maps scraping costs $2.85 per 1,000 businesses. If you want emails, that is another $3. If you want contacts with names, another $3. If you want phone enrichment, another $5. Add email verification on top and you are looking at $9 to $15 per 1,000 records before you have anything ready to send.

For teams doing occasional bulk scraping, the pay-as-you-go model is appealing. For agencies or freelancers who search daily and want decision maker emails with verified contacts, the math often tips in favor of a flat-rate alternative.

This guide compares Outscraper with 7 tools across pricing, decision maker data, complexity, and use case fit.

## FAQ: Outscraper Alternatives

**Q: Is Outscraper free?**

A: Outscraper gives you 500 businesses free per month on its free tier. Credits reset every 30 days. Enrichments (emails, contacts, phone) are not free and are charged at their per-record rates even on the free tier.

**Q: What is the best Outscraper alternative for non-developers?**

A: WebLeads for local business prospecting with decision maker emails. LeadSwift for broader data sources with outreach automation. D7 Lead Finder for basic volume at a flat rate. All three require no API configuration.

**Q: Can I get decision maker emails from Outscraper?**

A: Not directly. Outscraper's "Contacts and Leads Enrichment" finds general contacts associated with a domain. It does not specifically identify decision makers by name and role, and it does not run SMTP verification on the emails it finds. WebLeads is the tool in this comparison that provides decision maker discovery (name, role, verified email) as a core feature.

**Q: Is Outscraper more accurate than WebLeads?**

A: Both pull from Google Maps in real time. The difference is what they do after scraping. Outscraper gives you the raw record. WebLeads runs additional processing to find and verify decision maker email addresses. For cold email outreach, WebLeads contacts are more ready-to-use.

**Q: Which Outscraper alternative has the best free tier?**

A: Outscraper itself (500 businesses/month free). Apify ($5 compute credits free monthly). WebLeads (2 lifetime searches, 10 people enrichments, 20 verifications). Map Lead Scraper has no free tier.

**Q: Does Outscraper work for international markets?**

A: Yes. Outscraper scrapes Google Maps globally. WebLeads, Scrap.io, and Apify also support international searches. All tools in this comparison except D7 Lead Finder have global coverage.
