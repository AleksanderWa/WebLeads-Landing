# ZoomInfo Pricing Guide: Plans, Features & Cost Comparison

ZoomInfo is a popular B2B database used by sales teams, marketing departments, and recruiters. But what does it actually cost? And is the price worth it for your business?

This guide breaks down ZoomInfo pricing, explains what you get for each plan, and shows you how costs compare to alternative tools.

## What Is ZoomInfo?

ZoomInfo is a B2B database that compiles business contact information from public sources, online directories, and paid data aggregators. The platform serves three main use cases:

- **Sales prospecting**: Finding decision makers and companies in your target market
- **Marketing lists**: Building email lists for campaigns and lead generation
- **Recruiting**: Finding employee contact information for talent sourcing

## ZoomInfo Pricing Plans

ZoomInfo offers multiple pricing tiers based on your team size and use case.

### Sales Plus Plan

**Starting price: $99 per month** (for small sales teams)

The entry-level plan includes:

- Access to 150+ million business records
- Basic search filters (industry, company size, location)
- Up to 50 contact exports per month
- Email and phone verification
- CRM integration (limited)
- Email deliverability tools

Best for: Solo sales reps, small agencies, individual prospectors

### Sales Pro Plan

**Starting price: $349 per month** (for growing sales teams)

Includes everything in Sales Plus, plus:

- Up to 500 contact exports per month
- Advanced search filters and saved searches
- API access (limited)
- Team account management (up to 3 users)
- Priority support
- Integration with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive

Best for: Mid-size sales teams (3–10 people) who prospect daily

### Sales Enterprise Plan

**Custom pricing** (contact sales for quote)

Includes:

- Unlimited contact exports
- Unlimited saved searches
- Full API access
- Unlimited users
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom data enrichment

Best for: Large enterprises with dedicated sales operations teams

## What's Included in ZoomInfo Pricing?

### Contact Database

ZoomInfo claims 150+ million business records, but quality varies:

- Some records are decades old
- Duplicate records are common

You can search by:

- Company name and industry
- Job title and function

## Is ZoomInfo Worth the Price?

ZoomInfo is expensive for solo operators and small teams, but it offers value for large sales organizations because:

- **Breadth of data**: 150+ million records covers most mid-market and enterprise companies
- **Team collaboration**: Built for teams of 5+ who share leads and insights
- **Integrations**: Integrates with Salesforce and other enterprise tools

However, ZoomInfo is NOT worth it if:

- You are prospecting a niche industry (ZoomInfo focuses on broad coverage, not depth in specific verticals)
- You need highly localized businesses (contractors, HVAC, plumbers, etc.)
- You work with smaller businesses under 50 employees
- You cannot justify $349–$1,000 per month for contact data alone

## ZoomInfo Alternatives & Cost Comparison

### Hunter.io

**Pricing: $49–$499 per month**

Best for: Finding email addresses for specific people. Hunter uses domain scraping + machine learning to guess company emails.

Pros: Cheap, easy to use, good email accuracy

Cons: Limited company data, no phone numbers, no intent data

### Clay

**Pricing: $99–$749 per month**

Best for: Sales teams building custom data pipelines. Clay integrates with dozens of data sources.

Pros: Highly customizable, cheaper than ZoomInfo for niche searches

Cons: Requires technical setup, more of a tool than a database

### Clearbit

**Pricing: $400–$2,000 per month**

Best for: Marketing teams doing account-based marketing. Clearbit enriches data and provides intent signals.

Pros: High-quality company data, good intent data, strong integrations

Cons: Expensive, best for established teams, not ideal for cold prospecting

## How to Choose: ZoomInfo vs. Alternatives

Choose **ZoomInfo** if:
- You are a sales team prospecting mid-market or enterprise companies
- You need integration with Salesforce or HubSpot
- Your budget allows $5,000–$10,000/year per sales rep
- You want a single, unified platform for all contact data

Choose **an alternative** if:
- You prospect local businesses, contractors, or service providers
- You need lower costs ($100–$500 per month total)
- You want transparent, straightforward pricing with no surprises
- You value fresh data over database breadth

## Bottom Line

ZoomInfo is expensive but legitimate for large sales teams. If you are a sales leader at a 10+ person company, the $5,000–$10,000 annual cost per rep is justifiable.

But if you are smaller, bootstrap, or prospecting a specific niche, there are better options. Tools like WebLeads ($99 per month) and Hunter.io ($99 per month) give you 80% of the functionality at 20% of the cost.

For local businesses especially (contractors, HVAC, plumbers, real estate agents), WebLeads is the better choice because it searches Google Maps live and delivers verified, fresh data.
