## SECTION 11: FAQ

**Header:** Lusha vs WebLeads: Common Questions

**Q1: Can WebLeads replace Lusha entirely?**
A: For local business prospecting, yes. WebLeads does discovery and enrichment in one step. If you also need to enrich known LinkedIn contacts at an enterprise level, Lusha still has a role. The tools serve different workflows.

**Q2: Does WebLeads cover countries outside the US?**
A: Yes. WebLeads uses Google Maps, which covers businesses worldwide. Coverage is strongest where Google Maps is most actively used, including the US, UK, Canada, and Australia.

**Q3: How does WebLeads find decision maker emails if Lusha cannot?**
A: WebLeads finds the business website from Google Maps, generates likely email patterns for the owner, and runs SMTP verification on each until one connects. Lusha enriches contacts you already have. WebLeads finds contacts from scratch.

**Q4: What happens when a business has no website?**
A: Basic business data (name, address, phone, Google Maps info) is still returned. People enrichment requires a website domain to work. Businesses with no online presence cannot have decision maker emails found.

**Q5: Is WebLeads cheaper than Lusha?**
A: Yes. WebLeads starts at $24 per month for 1 search per day, 800 results per search, 500 people enrichments, and 3,000 email verifications. Lusha starts at $49.90 per month for 400 credits on a per-lookup model.

**Q6: Can I try WebLeads before paying?**
A: Yes. The free Discover plan gives you 2 lifetime searches and 10 people enrichments to test with real data before committing to a paid plan. No credit card required.
