# Apollo vs ZoomInfo: Which B2B Database Should You Use?

Both Apollo and ZoomInfo are popular B2B databases used by sales teams for lead generation and prospecting. But they serve different needs and come with very different price tags.

This guide compares Apollo and ZoomInfo side-by-side so you can decide which tool is best for your sales team.

## Quick Comparison: Apollo vs ZoomInfo

| Feature | Apollo | ZoomInfo |
|---------|--------|----------|
| **Pricing** | $49–$299/mo | $2,000+/mo |
| **Database Size** | 275M+ contacts | 700M+ contacts |
| **Email Accuracy** | 90–95% | 92–98% |
| **Sales Engagement** | Included | Add-on (RevBench) |
| **Setup Cost** | Minimal | High (implementation) |
| **Learning Curve** | Low | Steep |
| **Phone Numbers** | Limited | Extensive |
| **Target Audience** | Solo founders, SMBs | Enterprise, large teams |

## What Is Apollo?

Apollo is a B2B database and sales engagement platform designed for smaller sales teams and founders who need both prospecting data and outreach tools in one place.

**Core Features:**
- Access to 275M+ business contacts
- Email templates and sequences
- CRM with pipeline management
- Automated email tracking and follow-ups
- Basic integrations (Slack, Zapier, etc.)
- Starting price: $49/month

**Best for:** Solo founders, small sales teams, agencies growing from cold email

## What Is ZoomInfo?

ZoomInfo is an enterprise-grade B2B database designed for large sales organizations that need unlimited users, advanced integrations, and comprehensive support.

**Core Features:**
- Access to 700M+ contacts (larger database)
- Advanced account intelligence
- Integrations with major CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive)
- API access for custom integrations
- Dedicated account managers
- Starting price: $2,000+/month

**Best for:** Enterprises with 50+ sales reps, teams needing account-level intelligence

## Head-to-Head Comparison

### 1. Pricing & Cost Structure

**Apollo:** Transparent, per-user pricing
- Free Plan: $0 (100 exports/month)
- Basic: $49/month
- Professional: $165/month
- Custom/Enterprise: Contact sales

Apollo charges per user per month. A 5-person team costs ~$245/month on the Professional plan.

**ZoomInfo:** Enterprise pricing only
- Minimum contract: $2,000+/month
- Typically $5,000–$15,000/month for mid-size teams
- Add-ons for advanced features cost extra

ZoomInfo requires annual contracts and often doubles in cost with add-ons (RevBench for sales engagement, etc.).

**Winner:** Apollo. 10–100x cheaper, especially for small teams.

### 2. Database Size & Coverage

**Apollo:** 275M+ contacts across
- B2B companies worldwide
- Personal contacts from previous roles
- LinkedIn data (via integrations)

**ZoomInfo:** 700M+ contacts
- Larger database overall
- More phone numbers on file
- B2B account intelligence (who owns which company, titles, roles)

**Winner:** ZoomInfo for breadth, but Apollo's database is sufficient for most use cases.

### 3. Data Accuracy

**Apollo:** 90–95% email accuracy
- Verification via SMTP checks
- Daily updates from LinkedIn
- Some stale data in free tier

**ZoomInfo:** 92–98% email accuracy
- More frequent updates (instant)
- Higher accuracy because of enterprise funding and larger research team
- Costs reflected in pricing

**Winner:** ZoomInfo, but Apollo is still reliable for cold email.

### 4. Sales Engagement Tools

**Apollo:** Built-in
- Email templates
- Sequence automation
- Email open/click tracking
- Scheduling and follow-ups
- Included in monthly plan

**ZoomInfo:** Requires RevBench (separate tool/cost)
- Advanced orchestration
- Multi-channel sequences
- Account-based marketing
- Additional $1,000+/month

**Winner:** Apollo. Engagement tools included; ZoomInfo charges extra.

### 5. Integration Ecosystem

**Apollo:**
- Zapier, Slack, Microsoft Teams
- Google Sheets
- Basic CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Fewer native enterprise integrations

**ZoomInfo:**
- Deep Salesforce integration
- HubSpot, Pipedrive, Dynamics
- Slack, Outlook, Gmail
- Custom API access
- More enterprise-ready

**Winner:** ZoomInfo for large organizations with complex tech stacks.

### 6. Implementation & Setup

**Apollo:**
- Sign up in 5 minutes
- Start searching immediately
- No implementation needed
- Learning curve: 30 minutes

**ZoomInfo:**
- Requires onboarding call
- Implementation can take weeks
- Training for teams
- Ongoing support

**Winner:** Apollo for speed-to-value.

### 7. Use Cases & Ideal Customers

**Apollo is best if you:**
- Have a small sales team (1–10 reps)
- Need both data and outreach tools
- Want to control your own prospecting process
- Can't afford $2,000+/month
- Prefer simplicity over features
- Want to try lead generation without big investment

**ZoomInfo is best if you:**
- Have 50+ sales reps
- Need account-level intelligence
- Already have a large sales ops team
- Want vendor consolidation (one platform for everything)
- Can justify enterprise spend
- Need dedicated support and training

## Apollo Strengths vs ZoomInfo

### Apollo Advantages
- **Affordability:** 10–50x cheaper depending on team size
- **All-in-one:** Data + email outreach in one tool
- **Quick setup:** No implementation required
- **Founder-friendly:** Designed for SMBs and solo sellers
- **Email sequences:** Built-in automation saves tool sprawl

### ZoomInfo Advantages
- **Larger database:** 700M contacts vs 275M
- **Higher accuracy:** Especially for phone numbers
- **Enterprise scale:** Unlimited users, API access
- **Account intelligence:** Who owns what company, org charts
- **Integration depth:** Native to all major platforms
- **Professional support:** Dedicated account managers

## Which Should You Choose?

**Choose Apollo if:**
- Your budget is under $500/month
- You're a solo founder or small sales team
- You want an easy, all-in-one platform
- You prefer controlling your own prospecting

**Choose ZoomInfo if:**
- You have 50+ sales reps
- You need account-level intelligence
- You have a dedicated sales ops team
- Your budget is $5,000+/month
- You require dedicated support
