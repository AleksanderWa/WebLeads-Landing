## FAQ: Lusha Alternatives

**Q: What is the main difference between Lusha and WebLeads?**

Lusha is an enrichment tool. You need to already know who you are looking for. WebLeads is a discovery tool. You search by business type and location, and it finds the businesses plus their decision makers. Different workflows for different problems.

**Q: Does Lusha work for finding local business owners?**

Coverage for local SMBs (plumbers, contractors, salons, agencies) is limited in Lusha. These businesses often have minimal LinkedIn presence, which is where Lusha pulls most of its data. If local business discovery is your goal, a Google Maps based tool is more effective.

**Q: Which tool is cheapest for cold outreach to local businesses?**

WebLeads starts at $24 per month and includes 800 results per search with people enrichments and email verification. Lusha starts at $49.90 for 400 credits. For high-volume local prospecting, WebLeads delivers more per dollar.

**Q: Can I use Lusha and WebLeads together?**

Yes. Lusha works well for enriching known LinkedIn contacts in enterprise B2B. WebLeads handles local business discovery. They solve different problems and complement each other if your prospecting covers both use cases.

**Q: Does WebLeads work outside the US?**

Yes. WebLeads uses Google Maps as its data source and works in any country where Google Maps has business coverage. Most countries with active business directories are covered. Coverage is strongest where Google Maps is most used.

**Q: Which tool has the most accurate email data for local businesses?**

WebLeads uses SMTP verification on every decision maker email it finds before returning it. That means lower bounce rates than tools that pull emails from databases without live verification. Hunter.io and Snov.io also do SMTP verification but require you to supply the domain first.
