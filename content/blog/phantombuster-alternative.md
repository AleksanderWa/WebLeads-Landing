# 7 PhantomBuster Alternatives for Local Lead Generation (2026)

PhantomBuster is a flexible automation platform. You can scrape LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, and yes, Google Maps. The problem is that it is built around a concept called "Phantoms": individual automation scripts you configure one by one. For each data source, you pick a Phantom, set up cookies or API keys, configure session limits, and run it on a schedule.

That setup works well for LinkedIn power users who want to automate outreach sequences. For someone who just wants a list of local businesses with owner emails, it is a lot of machinery for a simple job.

This guide is for people searching PhantomBuster alternatives because they are doing local business prospecting, not social media automation. We compare 7 tools that are simpler, more focused, or better priced for finding local business leads.

## FAQ: PhantomBuster Alternatives

**Q: Does PhantomBuster have a Google Maps scraper?**

A: Yes. PhantomBuster has a "Google Maps Search Export" Phantom that extracts business listings. It works, but it is one of many Phantoms in the platform. It is not the primary focus, and the workflow requires more configuration than a purpose-built Maps scraping tool.

**Q: Which PhantomBuster alternative is best for Google Maps specifically?**

A: For Google Maps lead generation, WebLeads, Outscraper, and Scrap.io are purpose-built for it. PhantomBuster's Maps Phantom is a secondary feature on a platform designed primarily for LinkedIn and social media automation.

**Q: Is there a free PhantomBuster alternative?**

A: Yes. WebLeads has a free Discover tier (2 searches, 500 results per search, 100 people enrichments, 200 email verifications, all lifetime). Outscraper gives you 500 businesses free per month. Apify offers $5 in free compute credits monthly. Map Lead Scraper has a limited free tier.

**Q: Why is PhantomBuster so much more expensive than other tools?**

A: PhantomBuster prices based on automation hours ($56/mo for 20 hours). The model is designed for running long LinkedIn automation sequences, not quick Google Maps exports. For pure Maps scraping use cases, you are paying for functionality you do not need.

**Q: Does any tool in this list get decision maker emails from Google Maps results?**

A: WebLeads is the only tool in this comparison that combines Google Maps discovery with decision maker email finding. It identifies the business website domain, generates likely email patterns, and runs SMTP verification to return a confirmed working email. Outscraper and Scrap.io return business-level data without owner-specific email discovery.

**Q: What is the simplest PhantomBuster alternative for non-technical users?**

A: WebLeads and D7 Lead Finder are the most accessible. No API keys, no script configuration, no session management. Type your search, get results, export.
