# WebLeads Competitor Analysis

## Overview
This document contains detailed information about WebLeads competitors in the B2B lead generation space, particularly focusing on Google Maps scraping tools and local business data extraction platforms.

## Google Maps Scraping Tools

### 1. Map Lead Scraper
**Website**: https://www.mapleadscraper.com/
**Type**: Chrome extension
**Founded**: N/A

#### Pricing
- **Free Plan**: 1,000 leads/month, 15 records at a time, basic data extraction
- **Pro Monthly**: $19.90/month - 100,000 leads/month, unlimited exports, emails, social media
- **Pro Yearly**: $9.90/month (billed annually) - 100,000 leads/month, unlimited exports, emails, social media
- **Email Verification:** Not available

#### Features
- **Data Extraction**: Business names, phone numbers, emails, social media links, websites, addresses, categories, ratings, reviews, photos
- **Email Finding**: Visits each business website to find contact pages and email addresses (emails aren't on Google Maps but are usually on the business site)
- **Export Formats**: CSV/XLS files
- **Limitations**: Chrome extension only, limited to Google Maps
- **Target Users**: Individual users and small teams

#### Strengths
- Easy to use Chrome extension
- Real-time scraping
- Unlimited exports in paid plans
- Good for small-scale lead generation

#### Weaknesses
- Limited to Google Maps only
- No API access
- Basic filtering capabilities
- No enterprise features

#### Market Position
Budget-friendly option for individual users and small businesses doing occasional Google Maps scraping.

- **Email Verification**: $0.005 per email ($5.00 per 1,000 emails) - In-house SMTP verification
- **Phone Number Lookup**: $4.99 per 1,000 phones

#### Features
- **Data Extraction**: Comprehensive Google Maps data including emails, contacts, social media, reviews, ratings, photos, working hours, popular times, prices, menus
- **Export Formats**: CSV/Excel/Parquet/JSON
- **API Access**: Full API access with webhooks
- **Enrichments**: Website crawling for emails & contacts (separate pricing)
- **Advanced Filters**: Claimed status, reviews, ratings, photos, price range, occupancy, social media presence
- **No Search Limits**: Pay per record, not per search

#### Strengths
- No monthly commitments or recurring billing
- Credits never expire (prepaid model)
- Comprehensive data extraction (50+ data fields)
- API-first approach for developers
- Real-time data extraction
- Integrations with Zapier, HubSpot, etc.
- Trusted by enterprise clients
- Transparent pay-per-use pricing

#### Weaknesses
- Higher per-lead cost ($2.85 per 1,000 leads vs WebLeads transparent quota-based plans)

- Requires technical knowledge for optimal use
- Complex pricing with multiple service tiers
- No fixed monthly budget option

#### Market Position
API-first solution for developers and businesses that need programmatic access to Google Maps data without monthly commitments. Best for technical users who need comprehensive data extraction and integration capabilities, with unpredictable or sporadic usage patterns.

### 6. Map Lead Scraper (Why So Cheap?)
**Website**: https://www.mapleadscraper.com/
**Type**: Chrome extension
**Founded**: N/A

#### Pricing Analysis
Map Lead Scraper appears "cheap" at $9.90-$19.90/month for 1,000-100,000 leads, but this is misleading for several reasons:

#### Why Map Lead Scraper Pricing Seems Low
1. **Limited Scope**: Only scrapes basic Google Maps data (name, phone, address, website)
2. **No Advanced Features**: No email extraction, social media, reviews, ratings analysis
3. **Chrome Extension Limitations**: Can only run in browser, limited scalability
4. **Basic Filtering**: No advanced search filters or geolocation features
5. **No API Access**: Manual operation only, not suitable for automation

#### WebLeads Offers More Value Because:
1. **Comprehensive Data**: Extracts emails, social media, owner names, reviews, ratings
2. **Real-time Scraping**: Fresh data vs potentially cached results
3. **Advanced Features**: Website audits, SEO analysis, social presence analysis
4. **Multiple Data Sources**: Google Maps + YellowPages, Bing, Facebook, LinkedIn
5. **Outreach Automation**: Built-in email sequencing and follow-ups
6. **API Access**: Programmatic access for automation and integration
7. **Scalability**: Cloud-based platform vs browser extension limitations
8. **Support**: Professional support vs community-only

#### True Cost Comparison
- **Map Lead Scraper**: $9.90/month = $0.099 per 1,000 basic leads (limited data)
- **WebLeads**: Quota-based pricing ‚Äî Starter $29/mo (1 search/day, 800 results/search, 50 DM unlocks, 500 verifications); Growth $79/mo (3/day, 1,500 results, 200 DM, 2,000 verifications). Top-ups from $2 (DM) / $4 (verifications), 6-month validity.
- **Value Difference**: WebLeads provides full data, decision-makers, and pay-per-success verification at transparent quota-based pricing

#### Market Position
Budget option for users who only need basic contact information and are comfortable with manual, browser-based operation. Not suitable for serious lead generation or business automation needs.

## WebLeads Competitive Advantages

### 1. Specialized Focus
- **Unique positioning**: Only platform specialized in local business discovery via Google Maps
- **Target niche**: Local service providers, retailers, SMBs vs enterprise contacts

### 2. Transparent Pricing
- **Simple quota model**: Daily searches, monthly DM unlocks and email verifications ‚Äî clear limits per plan, optional top-ups (6-month validity)
- **Clear costs**: No hidden fees; pay-per-success for email verification (failed lookups cost $0)

### 3. Cost Advantage
- **Generous free trial**: Discover plan ‚Äî 2 searches (200 results each), 10 DM unlocks, 20 email verifications, no credit card required
- **Lower entry point**: Starter $29/mo vs $19.99‚Äì$49 for competitors
- **No annual contracts**

### 4. Technical Approach
- **Real-time scraping**: Fresh data vs potentially stale databases
- **No vendor lock-in**: Self-hosted capabilities available
- **API-first design**: Integration-friendly architecture

### 5. Flexibility
- **Unlimited categories**: Search any Google Maps business type
- **No search limitations**: Unlike competitors with daily search limits

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Maintain Specialized Positioning
Continue focusing on local business discovery as a differentiator from general B2B platforms.

### 2. Leverage Pricing Transparency
Emphasize the simple, predictable pricing model vs competitors' complex systems.

### 3. Expand Data Sources
Consider adding Bing Maps and other local directories to match LeadSwift's multi-source approach.

### 4. Develop Enterprise Features
Add team management, advanced analytics, and API access to compete with enterprise-focused competitors.

### 5. Content Marketing
Create comparison content highlighting cost advantages and feature parity with more expensive competitors.
