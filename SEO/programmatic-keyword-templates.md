## Template Structure Guide

**Variables Used:**
- `{industry}` = Business type (e.g., plumbers, restaurants, dentists)
- `{location}` = City, state, or region (e.g., Austin, Texas, San Francisco Bay Area)
- `{data_type}` = Type of contact info (e.g., emails, phone numbers, addresses, contacts)
- `{use_case}` = Business purpose (e.g., cold email, partnership outreach, market research)
- `{qualifier}` = Descriptive modifier (e.g., verified, active, local, b2b, quality)
- `{action}` = Verb describing what to do (e.g., find, get, buy, download, generate)
- `{intent}` = B2B signal words (e.g., leads, list, database, contacts, data)
